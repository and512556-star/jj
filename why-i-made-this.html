The purpose behind the Roblox Content Rating Board

RCRB Purpose & Process

Roblox has been facing increasing safety challenges, including inappropriate behavior from bad actors and the creation of unsafe or explicit games, often referred to as “condos” or Erotic Roleplay games. I created this rating system to help players and parents quickly understand the content and safety level of Roblox games.

A small review team will check around 3 games per day and report their findings to me, allowing each game to receive an appropriate rating based on its content, safety, and player experience.
